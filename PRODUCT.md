# Product

## Register

product

## Users
University students managing independent living. They are often context-switching between academic, social, and domestic tasks (bills, reminders, expenses).

## Product Purpose
A multi-utility "hub" that empowers students to manage their daily life with precision. Success is a user feeling they have total visibility and control over their scattered responsibilities in one minimal, friendly place.

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated, Premium, Lucid.

## Anti-references
Generic "basic" consumer templates, heavy/opaque card lists, "techie" developer-centric UIs (1px technical borders, mono fonts), and awkward, low-contrast theming.

## Design Principles
- **Lucid Sophistication**: Use a "Restrained" color strategy with a neutral base and a single, high-contrast, harmonious accent.
- **Invisible Hierarchy**: Prioritize bold typography and deep whitespace to group information. Avoid bounding boxes unless they add tactile value.
- **Tactile Glass**: Use semi-transparent, "glassy" surfaces and tonal layering to create a sense of premium depth and polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG compliance with a focus on high-contrast readability and generous tap targets for mobile-first utility.
